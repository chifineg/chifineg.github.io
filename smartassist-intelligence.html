SmartAssist Intelligence (SAI)

A concept for intelligent assistive technologies that can support safer ageing and independent living.

Overview

SAI considers how adaptive sensing and intelligent support could help older adults navigate daily routines with confidence.

Research objectives

Explore human-centred system requirements, safety-oriented assistance, and meaningful measures of autonomy.

Innovation

Bringing together assistive robotics, wearable sensing, and context-aware AI rather than treating them as isolated tools.

Expected impact

Support independence and help caregivers make timely, informed decisions without displacing human care.

Future development

Future work include co-design, prototype validation, privacy-preserving edge intelligence, and clinical evaluation.
